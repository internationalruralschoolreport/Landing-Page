Broadcast Your Needs

SchoolCast allows school administrators in the developing world to broadcast their needs to board members in the US who can help them.

School administrators

Running short on textbooks? Need updated computers? SchoolCast lets you share your needs with your board members who can help you get the resources you need to give students a high quality eduation.

Board members

SchoolCast lets you stay up to date on the needs of the international schools you support so you can donate your money effectively and help them acquire resources they actually need.

How it works

School administrators can login and create, read, update and delete request the supplies or repairs they need to teach effectively. Board members can log in and mark their request as done, scheduled, or ignored, and comment with more information.
